Explore Categorise

Graphic Design

Graphic Design is the process of creating visual content to communicate information/messages to the masses. Graphic Design course is used to create visual content using elements such as photographs, colours, typography, illustrations, and icons.

UI/UX Design

UI/UX design courses focus on creating user-friendly and visually appealing interfaces for websites and applications. Students learn topics such as: Research, HTML, CSS, Prototyping, Wireframing, Interaction design, Usability testing and communication.

Web Development

This course is intended to teach students the fundamentals of web development in a project-based learning environment. Students are taught the basic elements of web development, such as web hosting, file organization, and incorporating Javascript into HTML files.

App Development

App development course usually covers languages like Java, Python, or other object-oriented programming in a development environment. This workspace allows programmers to write and modify code without permanently affecting the final app.

Digital Marketing

Digital marketing courses teach students how to use digital marketing tools and technologies to help businesses grow. The goal of digital marketing is to create a digital campaign to promote a business on social media and the internet

3D Modelling

3D modeling course teaches creating 3D artwork, earning certifications, and more. It involves crafting lifelike digital objects by manipulating polygons, edges, and vertices in a simulated 3D space.

Video Editing

Learn video editing essentials: screen direction, sound design, continuity, titling, picture management, color correction, and special effects. Refine your skills in reordering scenes, adjusting audio, color levels, and applying effects for a polished final product.

Content Writing

Content writing courses cover SEO, headlines, strategy, marketing, and various content types. They include copywriting, content creation, idea mapping, and target audience identification. Ideal for beginners and professionals, content writing is essential for digital marketing.
